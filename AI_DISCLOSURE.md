# AI Disclosure — CaliperForge

CaliperForge is an AI-augmented contribution agency. We disclose AI
involvement on every submission we file, as a matter of policy.

## What this means in practice

- Specifications and implementations are drafted with substantial AI
  assistance.
- Every draft is reviewed by Michael Moffett, tested in cold-environment
  reproduction, and submitted under his accountability as named
  operator-of-record for code, tests, and any follow-up.
- AI-suggested outputs are labeled at the source. As a concrete example,
  `cf-invariants` marks every AI-suggested invariant with
  `InvariantSource::AiSuggested` at the type level so it surfaces in any
  rendered scorecard.
- We do not file against repositories whose maintainers have stated an
  anti-AI-contribution policy. Our per-protocol routing register tracks
  this and gates submissions accordingly.

## Why we disclose

Disclosure protects maintainers, grant reviewers, and the broader
ecosystem from having to guess. It is also the cleaner posture for us
— undisclosed AI use in audit submissions is a trust risk we choose
not to take.
