What this roadmap does not claim

  • We do not claim to find live exploits. Our Atlas cases are defender-side benchmark twins, not live exploit discoveries.
  • We do not claim formal verification. Our invariant harnesses are CI-enforced property tests, not proofs.
  • We do not claim coverage we have not shipped. Every “Shipped” item above has a public repo and CI receipt.
  • Items listed under “Proposing next” and “Pipeline” are not done. We will update this page when they ship.

Update cadence: this page updates when (a) a public artifact ships or (b) a major Atlas case ships. Status is current as of 2026-06-21.
