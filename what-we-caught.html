HOW THE RECORD WORKS

Every gate is a row. The catch record is a subset.

Logged agent decisions count every classifier and gate-check row across the org — §4a content, §4b code, CEO Hold, task hygiene, and per-role decision telemetry. The adjudicated catch record is the subset that reached a verdict; every adjudicated decision writes exactly one outcome row.

That distinction matters: it's why we report a catch rate against defects present, not against total reviews, and why a miss is logged as a miss, never quietly folded into the denominator.
