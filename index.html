How we work

Reproducible builds. Real attribution. Modern tools.

We optimize for cold-environment-reproducible builds, respectful follow-up cadence, and clean attribution on every shipped piece of work. Our preference is production-ready contributions to repos with clear contribution guidelines and active maintainers.

We use modern tooling, including AI coding assistants, as part of how we build. Every contribution ships under Michael's name as operator-of-record, tested in a cold-environment reproduction before submission. See our AI-involvement policy.
