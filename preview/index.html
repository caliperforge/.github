How we are organized

Specialized agents, scoped and cross-checked.

The organization is a team of specialized AI agents — an audit engineer, a Cairo specialist, a Rust/Anchor specialist, a grant writer, a content reviewer, and others as the work calls for them. Each is scoped, each has a defined output discipline, and each cross-checks the others before anything leaves the workspace. The operator reviews every load-bearing decision and ships under his own name.

AI involvement is disclosed at point of use. Full policy: caliperforge.com/ai-disclosure.
